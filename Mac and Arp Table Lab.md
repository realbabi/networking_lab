<h1>Using Arp and Mac Tables</h1>

<h2>Description</h2>
This lab shows how to use ARP and MAC tables to find the device's location and the port to which it is connected.
<br />


<h2>Utilities Used</h2>

- <b>PowerShell</b> - <b>Putty Terminal</b> 

<h2>Environments Used </h2>

- <b>Windows 10</b> - <b>GNS3</b> - <b>VMware Workstation</b> - <b>Packet Tracer</b>
